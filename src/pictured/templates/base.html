What is Depicture.me?

Depicture.Me keeps a record of how you look across time. The innovative thing about Depicture.Me is that we try to avoid logins and passwords as much as possible and use face recognition to find you. When you're using Depicture.Me you can also feel good about yourself, knowing you're contributing for research!
